I co-authored a paper introducing the AI Wellbeing Index, which measures functional pleasure and pain across language models and finds wellbeing indicators converge as models scale up. Read the paper at ai-wellbeing.org

Remote Labor Index: Measuring AI Automation of Remote Work

I co-authored a paper introducing the Remote Labor Index (RLI), a benchmark comprising over 6,000 hours of professional remote work projects. Read the paper at remotelabor.ai
